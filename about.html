The company

Bijamrit Foods Retail Pvt. Ltd.

A Bihar-based food company dedicated to bringing premium-quality makhana from the heart of India to homes across the country. Every batch is hygienically processed and carefully packed to ensure freshness and consistency.

Our vision

To make Bihar's world-famous makhana accessible to every Indian household while supporting local farmers and promoting healthy lifestyles — a trusted brand built on quality and authenticity.
